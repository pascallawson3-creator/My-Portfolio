My story

From the field in Benin to research in France

Field photo

I am a passionate Agronomist and Agricultural Advisor dedicated to driving sustainable farming systems, agroecological innovation, and food sovereignty. My expertise lies at the intersection of practical field experience, data-driven agricultural solutions, and strategic community leadership.

As Agricultural Advisor on the DURAGIRE project — a Netherlands-funded program — I support more than 150 farmers across 3 departments and 5 communes in Benin. As Senior Leader of the FSA English Club since 2023, I hold a C1 level in English and have led, trained, and mentored over 150 cross-disciplinary members and leaders.

Driven by continuous growth and resilience, I am always open to global research collaborations, international academic exchange, and impactful agribusiness initiatives.
